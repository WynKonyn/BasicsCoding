New Info on deliverables

We have realised that Friday deadlines don't work as effectively as people don't have time on a Friday to remedy any delays and the day is generally shorter. These deadlines all then get pushed to Monday mornings which are already chaos. So please note that all deadlines that were originally scheduled by me for a Friday are now on a Thursday. I have adapted these on Asana.

This email will look two weeks into the future with regards to deliverables so that they don't sneak up on anyone.

Please check out the new information in the deliverables tables. Do have a lookout for: a. Short Status update paragraph
b. Handover/Notify column - Who you must notify/ hand over to when complete
c.. Critical Task markers - This task is critical in keeping project on track d. Delay impacts - What effect does delaying this task have e. Dependencies - Who is depending on this task to complete their task down the line
